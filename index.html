Hey! However you found your way to this website: Welcome!

First, a few facts about me. I'm 22 years old, from Karlsruhe, and currently studying Computer Science in a dual program at SAP.

When I'm not sitting in front of my PC, I enjoy playing badminton and swimming.

Being able to program has always fascinated me. Ever since I first came into contact with it in my middle school computer science class, I haven't stopped. Looking back, I can say: It's not as hard to learn as I thought! My first project, an Android ordering app for the bar at a birthday party, might not have been beautifully designed or programmed, but it served its purpose. Over the years, the quality has improved with increasing projects and experience. On my GitHub you can find all my projects, including a chess algorithm, a customizable logger for .Net, and a website that visualizes sorting algorithms. In spring 2025, I started my homelab journey with Kubernetes and FluxCD.

I hope this website gives you an impression of me and my work. Feel free to contact me at info@marvin-fuchs.de!
